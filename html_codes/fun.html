Welcome to My Random Fun Facts Page

Did you know that octopuses have three hearts? Two pump blood to the gills, and the third keeps you wondering why you only have one. Also, when an octopus gets stressed, it might even eat one of its arms.

Imagine if humans did that during finals week!

Here’s another one: Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren’t. Let that sink in while you try to remember why there are three ‘r’s in ‘strawberry.’

Oh, and by the way, the inventor of the Pringles can is buried in one. Snack responsibly!
